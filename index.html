What is Key Club?

Key Club is a student-led high school organization, dedicated to provide service and leadership to many others. We strive to create an impact on the community no matter how big or small it is, growing as individuals and as a team with other members.

What is a divisional?

A divisional is the most important event that takes place in a Key Club division. Members and officers from around the entire island gather to discuss upcoming events and chat with people from different schools. There is one for each division every month, usually at the end of the month.

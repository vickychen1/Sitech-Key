Charity Miles

This app gives money to a charity of choice by giving 25 cents every mile you walk. All you have to do is download the app Charity Miles and join our team: NYDKC Division 10 and begin walking. All proceeds will go to The American Lung Association.

7 Cups

This is a site that allows those who are emotionally distressed to have a platform to talk about issues going on.

Steps to Take

  • Click here.
  • Press the cup icon and select volunteer as a listener.
  • Create an account.
  • Complete a 30-minute lesson where you will be trained to become an active listener.
  • Once completed you will be trained and can assist others.
